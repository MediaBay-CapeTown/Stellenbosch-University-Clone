How to Apply

Application Information

Follow these steps to submit a complete and competitive postgraduate application to the Department of Computer Science & Informatics.

Application Steps

01

Identify a Supervisor

Browse the supervisor list and contact potential supervisors to discuss your research interests. A supervisor must agree to work with you before you can apply.

02

Prepare Your Documents

Gather certified academic transcripts, a research proposal, CV, two academic references, and proof of English proficiency (if applicable).

03

Submit Online Application

Complete the SU postgraduate application form via the online portal. Upload all required documents and pay the application fee (R200).

04

Departmental Review

The postgraduate committee reviews your application and supervisor's recommendation. You may be invited for an interview or to present your research proposal.

05

Receive Offer & Register

Successful applicants receive a formal offer letter. Accept within 14 days and complete registration before the academic year begins in February.

Required Documents

Certified Academic Transcripts
All previous degrees, certified within 6 months
Research Proposal
2–8 pages depending on degree level
Curriculum Vitae (CV)
Academic and professional background
Two Academic References
From lecturers or supervisors who know your work
English Proficiency (if applicable)
IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 90+ for non-English medium

International Applicants

International students must provide certified equivalents of South African qualifications. SAQA evaluation required for non-South African degrees. Contact the International Office for guidance on visa and study permit requirements.
