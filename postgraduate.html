BSc Honours in Computer Science

NQF Level: 8

Duration: 1 year (full-time)

Total Credits: 128

Admission Requirements: A Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field with an average of at least 60% in final-year Computer Science modules.

The Honours programme provides advanced theoretical and practical knowledge in specialised areas of Computer Science. Students complete six coursework modules (16 credits each) and a 32-credit programming research project.

Honours Modules (16 credits each unless stated)

Code Module Name Semester Description
64947-712 Advanced Algorithms 1st Advanced design and analysis of algorithms, complexity theory, approximation algorithms and optimisation techniques.
64971-716 Vulnerability Discovery & Exploitation 1st Techniques for discovering software vulnerabilities and implementing defensive cybersecurity strategies.
63444-771 Honours Programming Project (32 credits) Year Independent supervised research project including literature review, implementation, evaluation and final dissertation.

MSc in Computer Science

NQF Level: 9

Duration: 1–2 years

Total Credits: 180

Admission Requirements: BSc Honours in Computer Science with at least 65% average.

The MSc programme is research-based. Students complete an approved research proposal and produce a full dissertation under supervision. Assessment is based on dissertation examination and oral defence.

PhD in Computer Science

NQF Level: 10

Duration: 3–4 years

Total Credits: 360

Admission Requirements: MSc in Computer Science or equivalent qualification.

The PhD programme requires the completion of original and publishable research contributing new knowledge to the field. Candidates must submit and defend a doctoral dissertation.
