The Best Jobs of 2024

For nearly 30 years CareerCast.me has published an annual "Best Jobs" ranking of 200 occupations using factors such as work environment, stress, salary and projected growth. In 2024 many positions closely related to Computer Science feature in the top‑10 — a reminder that computing skills remain highly valuable across many careers.

Top 10 (2024)

  1. Actuary
  2. Biomedical Engineer
  3. Software Developer
  4. Data Scientist
  5. Financial Analyst
  6. Mechanical Engineer
  7. Civil Engineer
  8. Marketing Manager
  9. Human Resources Manager
  10. Project Manager

Notably, Software Engineer did not appear in the top‑10 that year, though other sources continue to rank it highly — particularly in South Africa where roles such as Full Stack Developer (reported #1 locally), Data Analyst (#3), and Network Administrator (#7) are in high demand.

Why this matters for CS students

Even when a career title isn't explicitly a Computer Science role, programming and computational thinking are often extremely valuable — they increase productivity, enable automation, and open doors into data‑driven and technical management roles.

What about the future?

South African forecasting data is patchy, but international sources indicate sustained high demand for qualified Computer Scientists for the coming decade. For example, historical US data showed a 50% increase in software engineering headcount from 2003–2013 — much faster than overall job growth — and projections continue to show strong demand, low unemployment and above‑median salaries for many computing roles.
